# 30-Day Priorities

A rolling 30-day priority list to keep the team focused on the highest-leverage actions after launch.  
Review and update every Monday. Mark items complete with ✅.

---

## Week 1 — Foundation & Launch (Days 1–7)
*Focus: Generate first revenue, validate the offer, collect proof.*

- [ ] Complete Stripe compliance checklist
- [ ] Launch both storefronts with real payment links
- [ ] Send 3 pre-launch emails + 4 launch emails to existing list
- [ ] Post 5 social media posts (LinkedIn primary)
- [ ] DM 50 warm contacts directly (personalised, not mass)
- [ ] Collect first 3 testimonials or written reviews from buyers
- [ ] Log Week 1 metrics in `analytics/kpi_dashboard.csv`
- [ ] Confirm cart abandonment emails are triggering correctly

**Goal:** First $2,000 in revenue. 5+ Micro-Coaching subscribers.

---

## Week 2 — Amplify & Optimise (Days 8–14)
*Focus: Paid acquisition, conversion optimisation, and social proof.*

- [ ] Launch $10–$20/day Facebook/Instagram retargeting campaign
- [ ] A/B test 2 ad creative variants from `marketing/social-and-ads/ad-copy.md`
- [ ] Launch Google Search ad for "sales objection training" keyword cluster
- [ ] Publish first LinkedIn article (repurpose one email into a long-form post)
- [ ] Collect 5 testimonials — add to `landing.html` on both storefronts
- [ ] Review and optimise landing page based on scroll depth + CTR data from GA4
- [ ] Identify 3 sales influencers or podcast hosts for affiliate outreach
- [ ] Send first affiliate outreach email to each
- [ ] Log Week 2 metrics

**Goal:** Paid ads ROAS > 2×. 15+ total subscribers. $4,000 cumulative revenue.

---

## Week 3 — Deepen & Expand (Days 15–21)
*Focus: Content depth, community building, and B2B pipeline.*

- [ ] Publish first blog post / SEO content piece (topic: "How to handle the 5 most common B2B objections")
- [ ] Host first subscriber-only live Q&A (30 min, Zoom or LinkedIn Live)
- [ ] Create and share a "Week 2 results" transparency post on LinkedIn (builds trust + social proof)
- [ ] Add a "Team Licences" CTA to both landing pages
- [ ] Start building a B2B prospect list (companies with 5–20 person sales teams)
- [ ] Send first B2B outreach email to 10 prospects
- [ ] Review churn — send a re-engagement email to any subscriber who hasn't opened in 14 days
- [ ] Upsell existing Course 01 buyers on Course 02 via a personal email
- [ ] Log Week 3 metrics

**Goal:** 25+ subscribers. First B2B conversation booked. $6,000 cumulative revenue.

---

## Week 4 — Review, Scale & Plan Month 2 (Days 22–30)
*Focus: Accountability, optimisation, and planning the next 30 days.*

- [ ] Complete full Month 1 KPI review:
  - Total revenue
  - MRR
  - Active subscribers
  - Churn rate
  - CAC
  - LTV estimate
  - Best-performing channel
- [ ] Increase ad spend to $30–$50/day if ROAS > 3×
- [ ] Close or advance at least 1 B2B corporate deal
- [ ] Onboard first affiliate partner (minimum 1)
- [ ] Plan Month 2 email sequence (3 emails: value, social proof, new offer)
- [ ] Plan Month 2 content calendar (12 LinkedIn posts, 2 blog articles)
- [ ] Draft Month 2 product extension: Course 04 outline or Micro-Coaching Pro tier spec
- [ ] Send NPS survey to all customers (use Typeform free tier or Google Form)
- [ ] Update `analytics/kpi_dashboard.csv` with final Month 1 data
- [ ] Share Month 1 retrospective with team/advisor

**Goal:** $8,000+ Month 1 total revenue. 30+ subscribers. Month 2 plan locked.

---

## Standing Weekly Actions (Every Week)

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| Monday | Review last week's KPIs. Update `kpi_dashboard.csv`. Plan this week's priorities. |
| Tuesday | Post LinkedIn content (#1 of week). DM 10 warm contacts. |
| Wednesday | Review ad performance. Adjust budget or creative if needed. |
| Thursday | Post LinkedIn content (#2 of week). Follow up on open sales conversations. |
| Friday | Reply to all comments/DMs. Write next week's 2 LinkedIn posts. EOD wrap-up. |
