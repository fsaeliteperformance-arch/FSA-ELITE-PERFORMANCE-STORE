## Brand Voice Guidelines

| Dimension | Description |
|---|---|
| **Tone** | Direct, confident, no-fluff. Speaks rep-to-rep, not coach-to-student. |
| **Language** | Plain English. No corporate jargon. Use numbers and specifics whenever possible. |
| **Personality** | The best closer you know — who actually wants to help you win. |
| **What we avoid** | Hype without substance. Vague promises. Motivational posters masquerading as training. |
| **What we say** | "Here's the script." "Here's why it works." "Here's the result it got." |
