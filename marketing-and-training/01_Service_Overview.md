# 01 — Service Overview

## What FSA Elite Performance Does

FSA Elite Performance provides practical, no-nonsense sales training and resources for individual contributors, sales leaders, and companies that want to close more deals — starting this week.

We do **not** sell:
- Motivational content with no actionable takeaways
- Generic "mindset" courses that don't translate to real calls
- Expensive consulting retainers that take months to deliver results

We **do** sell:
- Word-for-word scripts for every objection, call type, and deal stage
- Done-for-you playbook templates your team can implement immediately
- Live group coaching for reps who want continuous improvement at an accessible price point
- Physical tools (workbooks, branded gear) that reinforce daily execution habits

## Ideal Customer Profiles

**ICP 1 — The Hungry SDR**  
- 0–2 years experience
- Struggling with cold call objections and booking meetings
- Budget-conscious; wants high ROI for personal development spend
- Pain: "I freeze when someone says 'not interested.'"

**ICP 2 — The Closing AE**  
- 2–5 years experience
- Getting good demos but losing deals at the close
- Wants frameworks for negotiation and late-stage objections
- Pain: "I get to the proposal stage and then deals go cold."

**ICP 3 — The Sales Manager Who Can't Train**  
- 5+ years, managing a team of 3–15 reps
- No budget for a full training programme
- Wants something their team will actually use
- Pain: "Every training we've tried just collects dust."
