### 1. Scope of Work

FSA Elite Performance will deliver a custom sales playbook for [Company Name]'s sales team of [X] reps.

**Deliverables:**
1. ICP definition and messaging matrix (2 ICPs minimum)
2. Cold call script library (SDR and AE variants)

4. Objection handling scripts (minimum 8 objections)
5. Follow-up email cadence (5-touch sequence)
6. Pipeline stage definitions and exit criteria
7. 30-60-90-day onboarding plan for new reps
8. One live 60-minute training session with the team to walk through the playbook

**Format:** Google Docs (editable) + PDF export
