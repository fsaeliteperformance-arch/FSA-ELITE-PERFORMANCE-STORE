# 04 — Coaching & KPIs

## FSA Elite Performance — Coaching Framework & Key Performance Indicators

This document defines the coaching methodology used in Micro-Coaching sessions and provides KPI benchmarks for FSA Elite reps and clients.

---

## Coaching Philosophy

FSA Elite coaching is built on three principles:

1. **Specificity over generality.** Every coaching interaction focuses on a specific call, deal, or behaviour — not abstract concepts.
2. **Script-first.** Every framework is delivered with a word-for-word script the rep can use on their next call.
3. **Accountability.** Every session ends with a commitment from the rep: one action they will take before the next session.

---

## Coaching Session Structure (60-minute Group Call)

| Time | Activity | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 min | Check-in & wins | Build confidence, acknowledge progress |
| 5–20 min | Hot seat: one rep's live deal or call | Peer learning, real-world application |
| 20–40 min | Theme deep-dive (monthly topic) | Teach new framework or script |
| 40–50 min | Role-play practice | Skill practice in a safe environment |
| 50–58 min | Q&A | Address specific challenges |
| 58–60 min | Commitment round | Each rep states their one action for the week |

## Coaching Conversation Frameworks

### Framework 1 — Call Debrief (5-minute post-call review)
Use after any call — cold call, discovery, demo, or close attempt.

1. **What was your goal for this call?**
2. **What happened? Walk me through it.**
3. **Where did you feel you lost control or momentum?**
4. **What would you say differently next time?**
5. **What's the one script change you'll make?**

### Framework 2 — Deal Review (10-minute pipeline review)
Use for stuck deals or deals in the final stage.

1. **Who is the economic buyer? Have you spoken to them directly?**
2. **What is the compelling event? (Why now vs. Q2?)**
3. **What objections are still alive? Which have been fully resolved?**
4. **What is the next agreed step? (Who does what by when?)**
5. **What is the worst-case and best-case scenario for this deal?**

### Framework 3 — Weekly 1:1 (15-minute manager-rep check-in)
Use for regular manager-rep coaching cadence.

1. **What was your biggest win this week?**
2. **What was your biggest challenge?**
3. **What's in your pipeline that needs attention?**
4. **What skill do you want to improve this week?**
5. **What support do you need from me?**
