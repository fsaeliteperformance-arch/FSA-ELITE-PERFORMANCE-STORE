## Case Study Collection Checklist

To collect a case study:

1. **Identify:** A customer who has achieved a measurable result within 30 days of purchase.
2. **Email:** Send the "Case Study Request" email (template below).
3. **Interview:** 10-minute call or async video (Loom).
4. **Draft:** Fill in this template based on the interview.
5. **Approve:** Send the draft to the client for approval before publishing.
6. **Publish:** Add the condensed quote to the landing page and schedule a LinkedIn post.
