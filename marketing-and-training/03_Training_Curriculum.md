# 03 — Training Curriculum

## FSA Elite Performance — Full Training Curriculum

This document outlines all current and planned training modules, organised by skill level and sales role.

## Curriculum Map

```
Level 1: Foundations (SDR / New Rep)
    ├── Module 1.1: Sales Mindset & Identity
    ├── Module 1.2: Prospecting & ICP Definition
    ├── Module 1.3: Cold Call Fundamentals
    └── Module 1.4: Email & LinkedIn Outreach

Level 2: Core Skills (SDR to AE)
    ├── Module 2.1: Discovery & Qualification (SPIN + MEDDIC)
    ├── Module 2.2: Objection Handling (Objection Masterclass ← LIVE)
    ├── Module 2.3: Demo & Presentation Skills
    └── Module 2.4: Proposal & Pricing Conversations

Level 3: Closing & Advanced (AE)
    ├── Module 3.1: Closing Frameworks & Techniques
    ├── Module 3.2: Negotiation & Multi-Stakeholder Deals
    ├── Module 3.3: Enterprise Selling (coming Q2)
    └── Module 3.4: Account Expansion & Upsells

Level 4: Leadership (Head of Sales / Manager)
    ├── Module 4.1: Building a Sales Playbook (Sales Playbook Starter ← LIVE)
    ├── Module 4.2: Hiring & Onboarding Reps
    ├── Module 4.3: Pipeline Management & Forecasting
    └── Module 4.4: Coaching Reps to Performance
```
