## Email 2 — Value Add (Send 3 days after Email 1, if no reply)

**Subject:** The objection that kills most deals (and how to handle it)

---

Hey [First Name],

Following up quickly — wanted to share something you might find useful regardless of whether we talk.

The most deal-killing objection I see:

**"Send me more information and I'll get back to you."**

Most reps say "Of course!" and lose the deal.

Here's what works instead:

*"Absolutely — what specifically would be most useful for me to include? And what would it take for that information to move things forward on your end?"*

This does three things:
1. Keeps the conversation going instead of ending the call
2. Qualifies what the prospect actually needs to decide
3. Sets a commitment before you even send anything
