## Partnerships & Affiliate Programme

### Phase 1 (Month 1–3): Informal Referrals
- Offer existing customers a **15% affiliate commission** on any referral that converts.
- Track via unique Stripe Payment Link per affiliate (free, no software required).
- Referral commission paid monthly via PayPal or bank transfer.

### Phase 2 (Month 3–6): Formal Affiliate Programme
- Set up affiliate software (Rewardful, $49/month, native Stripe integration).
- Recruit sales influencers, LinkedIn creators, and sales podcast hosts as affiliates.
- Commission structure: 20% on one-time courses, 15% recurring on subscriptions.

### Phase 3 (Month 6+): B2B Partnerships
- Partner with sales enablement platforms (Outreach, Salesloft, Gong) for co-marketing.
- Bundle FSA Elite courses into corporate training packages sold directly to companies.
- Target: 3–5 corporate clients at $2,000–$10,000/year each.
