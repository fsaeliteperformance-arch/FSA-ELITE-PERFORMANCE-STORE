## Description
The **Sales Playbook Starter** gives new and scaling sales teams the foundational playbook they never had time to write.  
Includes a fully editable Google Docs / Notion template, a live screen-share walkthrough, and 5 fill-in-the-blank call frameworks for cold calls, discovery, and follow-ups.

---

## Learning Outcomes
1. Build a ready-to-use 30-60-90-day onboarding playbook for new reps.
2. Write a compelling cold-call opener in under 5 minutes.
3. Design a 5-step discovery call flow tailored to your ICP.
4. Create pipeline stage definitions and exit criteria.
5. Build a follow-up cadence that converts without annoying prospects.
