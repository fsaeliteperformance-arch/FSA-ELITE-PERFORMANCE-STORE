## Description
The **Micro-Coaching Subscription** is FSA Elite's ongoing coaching membership.  
Members get access to one live 60-minute group coaching call per month (recorded), a private community of peers, and a growing library of bite-sized training clips (5–15 min each), updated monthly based on member requests.

## What Members Get Each Month
