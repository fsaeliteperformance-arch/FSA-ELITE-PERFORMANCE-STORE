Affiliates of **FSA Elite Sales Training** (a brand of Fontenots Sales Association LLC) must adhere to the following brand and promotion restrictions:

- You may **only** promote FSA Elite Sales Training products and the **FSA Elite Sales App** through pre-approved channels and creatives.
- Do **not** use the brand names "FSA Elite Sales Training," "Fontenots Sales Association LLC," or "FSA Elite Sales App" in any domain names, social media handles, or app store listings without prior written approval from Kaygun Fontenot.
- Do **not** make income claims, earnings projections, or guarantees on behalf of FSA Elite Sales Training without written approval.
- Do **not** use misleading, deceptive, or comparative advertising that disparages competitors.
- Do **not** bid on brand-specific keywords (e.g., "FSA Elite," "FSA Elite Sales App") in paid search advertising without written approval.
- All promotional content must include the required FTC affiliate disclosure (see Section 11).
- Merchandise and physical goods bearing FSA Elite branding require separate written approval from Kaygun Fontenot before production or sale.

## 2. Eligibility

To participate in the FSA Elite Sales Training affiliate program you must:

1. Be 18 years of age or older (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction).
2. Have an active website, social media presence, newsletter, or other legitimate marketing channel relevant to sales training, personal development, or business growth audiences.
3. Submit a complete affiliate application and receive written approval from Fontenots Sales Association LLC.
4. Agree to these Terms in full and comply with all applicable laws, including FTC endorsement guidelines.
5. Not be a current employee or contractor of Fontenots Sales Association LLC without separate written consent.

Fontenots Sales Association LLC reserves the right to reject or revoke affiliate status at its sole discretion.

## 4. Attribution & Tracking

- Affiliate tracking is performed via unique affiliate links and/or coupon codes assigned at enrollment.
- Cookie window: **30 days** from first click. Last-click attribution applies unless otherwise specified.
- If a customer clears cookies or uses a different browser/device, the sale may not be attributed to the referring affiliate.
- Affiliates must not use redirects, iframes, or link-cloaking methods that obscure the affiliate tracking parameter without prior approval.
- Self-referrals (purchasing through your own affiliate link) are strictly prohibited and will result in commission reversal and possible termination.

---

## 5. Payouts & Cut-off

- **Payout threshold:** Minimum balance of $50.00 USD before a payout is issued.
- **Payment schedule:** Monthly, processed on or before the 15th of the month following the qualifying period.
- **Cut-off date:** Sales must be confirmed and non-refunded as of the last calendar day of the preceding month to be included in the next payment cycle.
- **Payment methods:** PayPal, ACH (US affiliates), or wire transfer (international affiliates, subject to wire fees).
- Affiliates are responsible for all applicable taxes. A completed W-9 (US) or W-8BEN (international) form is required before first payout.

---

## 6. Refunds & Clawback

- FSA Elite Sales Training offers a **14-day refund policy** on course products and a **7-day refund policy** on FSA Elite Sales App subscriptions, subject to the terms posted on the product page.
- If a referred customer receives a full or partial refund, the corresponding commission will be **clawed back** (deducted from the affiliate's pending or future balance).
- Chargebacks resulting from referred sales will also result in commission reversal plus a $15 chargeback-handling fee deducted from the affiliate's account.

---

## 7. Fraud & Compliance

The following activities constitute fraud and will result in immediate termination and forfeiture of all pending commissions:

- Cookie stuffing, forced clicks, or any artificial inflation of click or conversion counts.
- Generating fake leads, orders, or accounts.
- Using bots, scripts, or automated tools to simulate traffic or conversions.
- Violating any applicable spam law (CAN-SPAM, CASL, GDPR, etc.) when sending promotional emails.
- Engaging in any other deceptive or illegal marketing practice.

Fontenots Sales Association LLC reserves the right to pursue legal action and seek damages for fraudulent activity.
