## Part 1 — KPI Dashboard Suggestions

The following KPIs are recommended for tracking on the FSA Elite performance dashboard (internal and affiliate-facing reporting). These metrics give Kaygun Fontenot and the Fontenots Sales Association LLC team a clear picture of business health, affiliate performance, and product adoption.

---

### Section A — Revenue & Sales KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) | Total active subscription revenue per month | [Set per business target] |
| Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) | MRR × 12 | [Set per business target] |
| Course Revenue (One-Time) | Total revenue from FSA Elite Sales Training Course purchases | Track month-over-month |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Total revenue ÷ number of orders | [Benchmark after first 90 days] |
| Revenue by Product | Breakdown by Course, Starter, Growth, Performance | Monitor for upsell opportunity |
| Refund Rate | Refunds ÷ total sales (by product) | Target: < 5% |
| Chargeback Rate | Chargebacks ÷ total transactions | Target: < 0.5% |

---

### Section B — Conversion & Funnel KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Landing Page Conversion Rate | Visitors who complete purchase ÷ total landing page visitors | [Benchmark after first 30 days] |
| Checkout Completion Rate | Completed checkouts ÷ checkout sessions initiated | Target: > 70% |
| Free Trial Start Rate | Trial starts ÷ pricing page visitors | [Set after trial launch] |
| Trial-to-Paid Conversion Rate | Paid conversions ÷ trial starts | Target: > 30% |
| Cart Abandonment Rate | Abandoned carts ÷ total cart creates | Target: < 60% |
| Cart Recovery Rate | Recovered carts ÷ abandoned carts (via email/chat) | Target: > 15% |

---

### Section C — Retention & Engagement KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Churn Rate | Cancellations ÷ active subscribers at start of month | Target: < 5% |
| Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) | Average revenue per customer over subscription lifetime | Track by plan tier |
| LTV : CAC Ratio | Customer Lifetime Value ÷ Customer Acquisition Cost | Target: > 3:1 |
| Course Completion Rate | Students who complete full course ÷ total enrolled | Target: > 40% |
| App Daily Active Users (DAU) | Unique users who log in per day | Track trend weekly |
| Net Promoter Score (NPS) | Surveyed customer willingness to recommend FSA Elite | Target: > 50 |

---

### Section D — Affiliate Program KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Active Affiliates | Affiliates with at least 1 referred sale in past 30 days | [Set per growth target] |
| Affiliate-Attributed Revenue | Revenue from affiliate-referred purchases | Track as % of total revenue |
| Average Commission per Affiliate | Total commissions paid ÷ active affiliates | Benchmark after first 60 days |
| Affiliate Activation Rate | Affiliates who generate ≥1 sale ÷ total approved affiliates | Target: > 40% |
| Affiliate Refund Rate | Commissions clawed back ÷ total commissions earned | Monitor for fraud signals |
| Brand Approval SLA | % of brand approval requests responded to within 5 business days | Target: 100% |

---

### Dashboard Display Recommendations

- Display all revenue KPIs at the top (executive summary view).
- Include a 30/60/90-day trend line for MRR, conversion rate, and churn.
- Affiliate KPIs should be visible to Kaygun Fontenot and the program administrator in a separate affiliate management tab.
- Send a weekly automated KPI digest to Kaygun Fontenot and the Fontenots Sales Association LLC leadership team.
- Flag any metric that deviates more than 20% from the 30-day moving average for review.

## Part 2 — Case Study Framework

Use this framework to build FSA Elite Sales Training customer case studies for use in marketing, sales, and affiliate promotional materials (subject to standard brand approval process).

---

### Case Study Structure

---

#### 1. Customer Profile

- **Name:** [Customer First Name, Last Initial or full name with consent]
- **Role / Title:** [e.g., Account Executive, Sales Manager, Business Owner]
- **Industry:** [e.g., SaaS, Real Estate, Insurance, B2B Services]
- **Experience Level at Time of Enrollment:** [e.g., 2 years in sales, new rep, seasoned veteran]

---

#### 2. The Challenge (Before FSA Elite)

*Describe the specific problem the customer was facing before enrolling in FSA Elite Sales Training or using the FSA Elite Sales App.*

**Prompts:**
- What was their biggest sales challenge?
- Were they missing quota? Struggling with objections? Lacking a system?
- What had they already tried that didn't work?

---

#### 3. The Solution (FSA Elite Sales Training / FSA Elite Sales App)

*Describe specifically which FSA Elite products the customer used and how.*

**Prompts:**
- Did they take the course, use the app, or both?
- Which plan (Starter, Growth, Performance)?
- Did they participate in group coaching calls or 1-on-1 coaching with Kaygun Fontenot?
- Which specific lessons, scripts, or tools did they use most?

---

#### 4. The Results (After FSA Elite)

*Quantify the results wherever possible.*

**Prompts:**
- What changed in their numbers? (e.g., close rate, monthly revenue, pipeline size)
- How long did it take to see results?
- What was their biggest personal win?

**Example format:**
> "After completing the FSA Elite Sales Training Course and using the Growth Pack for 60 days, [Customer Name] increased their close rate from [X]% to [Y]% and added $[Z] in monthly commissions."

---

#### 5. The Quote

*A short, direct testimonial from the customer in their own words.*

> "[Two to four sentences capturing their experience and result, in their voice.]"
> — [Customer Name], [Title], [Industry]

---

#### 6. CTA (for use in marketing)

> "Ready to get results like [Customer First Name]? Enroll in FSA Elite Sales Training today or try the FSA Elite Sales App free for [X] days."  
> **[Enroll Now]** | **[Start Free Trial]**

---

### Case Study Usage & Approval

- All published case studies must be reviewed and approved by Kaygun Fontenot or Fontenots Sales Association LLC before publication.
- Customer must provide written consent before their name, photo, or company is used in any case study.
- Affiliates may share approved case studies verbatim with the required FTC disclosure. Affiliates may not modify or add to case study content without re-approval.
- Do not use case studies to imply guaranteed results or specific earnings.
