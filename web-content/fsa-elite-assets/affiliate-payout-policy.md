# FSA Elite Sales Training — Affiliate Payout Policy

**Brand:** FSA Elite Sales Training | FSA Elite Sales App  
**Entity:** Fontenots Sales Association LLC  
**Owner:** Kaygun Fontenot  

---

## Affiliate Payout Policy Statement

The following payout policy applies to all active affiliates of the **FSA Elite Sales Training** affiliate program, operated by **Fontenots Sales Association LLC** under the authority of **Kaygun Fontenot**.

---

### Payout Schedule

Affiliate commissions are paid **monthly**, on or before the **15th day of the month** following the qualifying sales period.

*Example: Commissions earned in January (confirmed and non-refunded as of January 31) are paid on or before February 15.*

---

### Minimum Payout Threshold

A minimum account balance of **$50.00 USD** must be accumulated before a payout is issued. Balances below this threshold roll over to the following month.

---

### Cut-Off Date

Sales must be:

1. **Confirmed** (payment successfully processed by Stripe or the designated payment processor), and
2. **Non-refunded** (outside the applicable refund window — 14 days for course products, 7 days for FSA Elite Sales App subscriptions)

as of the **last calendar day of the preceding month** to be included in that month's payout.

---

### Payment Methods

| Method | Availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| PayPal | All affiliates | PayPal fees may apply |
| ACH Direct Deposit | US affiliates only | 2–3 business day processing |
| International Wire Transfer | Non-US affiliates | Wire fees deducted from payout |

Affiliates must provide accurate payment details in the affiliate portal. Fontenots Sales Association LLC is not responsible for misdirected payments due to incorrect account information supplied by the affiliate.

---

### Tax Documentation

All affiliates must submit a completed tax form before their first payout:

- **US affiliates:** Form W-9
- **Non-US affiliates:** Form W-8BEN or applicable equivalent

Payouts will not be issued until the required tax form is on file.

---

### Refunds & Clawbacks

- If a referred customer receives a refund, the associated commission will be **reversed** (clawed back) from the affiliate's pending or future balance.
- Chargebacks will also result in commission reversal plus a **$15.00 USD chargeback-handling fee** deducted from the affiliate's account.

---

### Changes to Payout Policy

Fontenots Sales Association LLC reserves the right to modify this payout policy with **14 days' written notice** to active affiliates.
