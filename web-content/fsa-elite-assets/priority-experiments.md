## Priority A/B Experiment List

The following 7 experiments are prioritized for immediate execution based on expected impact on conversion rate, average order value (AOV), and affiliate activation rate.

---

### Experiment 1 — Hero Headline Variant Test (Landing Pages)

**What:** Test the primary hero headline on the FSA Elite Sales Training course landing page and the FSA Elite Sales App SaaS landing page.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** Current headline
- **Variant A:** Pain-focused headline (e.g., "Tired of Losing Deals You Should Have Won?")
- **Variant B:** Outcome-focused headline (e.g., "Close More Deals. Earn More. Start Today.")

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

### Experiment 2 — CTA Button Copy Test (Pricing Page)

**What:** Test primary CTA button copy on the pricing page across all plan cards.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** "Get Started"
- **Variant A:** "Start Free Trial"
- **Variant B:** "Activate My Plan"
- **Variant C:** "Start Closing More"

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

### Experiment 3 — Free Trial vs. No Free Trial Offer (Starter & Growth Plans)

**What:** Test whether displaying a free trial offer vs. a direct purchase offer on the Starter and Growth plan cards produces a higher conversion rate and lower churn.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** Direct purchase (no trial messaging)
- **Variant A:** 7-day free trial prominently displayed
- **Variant B:** "Try it free for 7 days, then [price]/month"

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

### Experiment 4 — Social Proof Placement Test (Product Pages)

**What:** Test whether placing customer testimonials above vs. below the feature list on the FSA Elite Sales Training Course product page improves conversion.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** Testimonials below features
- **Variant A:** Testimonials above features (directly below hero)
- **Variant B:** Testimonials in sidebar / alongside features

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

### Experiment 5 — Pricing Page Plan Recommendation Label

**What:** Test whether adding a "Most Popular" or "Best Value" badge to one plan card increases selection of that plan.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** No badge on any plan
- **Variant A:** "Most Popular" badge on Growth Pack
- **Variant B:** "Best Value" badge on Performance Pack (annual)
- **Variant C:** "Recommended" badge on Growth Pack

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

### Experiment 6 — Checkout Trust Badge Placement

**What:** Test whether moving security / guarantee trust badges above vs. below the checkout form increases checkout completion rate.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** Trust badges below the purchase button
- **Variant A:** Trust badges directly above the purchase button
- **Variant B:** Trust badges in the right column alongside the order summary

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

### Experiment 7 — Affiliate Onboarding Email Subject Line Test

**What:** Test which subject line variant produces the highest open rate and click-through rate for the affiliate onboarding email.

**Variants:**
- **Control:** "Welcome to the FSA Elite Affiliate Program — Your Next Steps Inside"
- **Variant A:** "You're In! Here's How to Start Earning with FSA Elite Sales Training"
- **Variant B:** "Your FSA Elite Affiliate Account Is Ready — Let's Get Started"

*Full success criteria and estimated impact in original bundle.*

---

## Implementation Notes

- Each experiment should be run to statistical significance (minimum 95% confidence) before declaring a winner.
- Run one test per page at a time to avoid interaction effects.
- Minimum test duration: 2 weeks regardless of early results.
- Document all results in the FSA Elite performance tracker and report to Kaygun Fontenot / Fontenots Sales Association LLC upon completion.
